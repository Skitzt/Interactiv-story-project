Embrace Hard Work

Adapting to his chosen educational path, Oliver faces the inevitable hardships and setbacks that come with climbing the ladder of social mobility. Faced with mounting obstacles, he must decide whether to embrace hard work and persevere or succumb to the weight of despair that threatens to extinguish his dreams.

Succumb to Despair

Oliver's heart, heavy with the burden of challenges, weighs on him like an anchor shackled to his dreams. With each passing day, despair, a treacherous undertow, threatens to pull Oliver beneath the waves of defeat, eroding his spirit with each passing wave. Oliver's struggles with the weight of his choices and the magnitude of his determination. .

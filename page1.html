High School

Private school

Oliver receives a scholarship offer to attend a prestigious private school, located in the bustling city of London.
On the one hand, private schools like Eton College offer an exclusive and elitist educational experience.
The private school Oliver is offered admission to is situated on a picturesque countryside estate, surrounded by vast green landscapes.
However, the financial burden and limited access to those from less affluent backgrounds pose significant challenges.

Public school

Alternatively, Oliver's neighborhood is served by a public school called Waterford Secondary School.
This school is located in a modest building, surrounded by the rowhouses and community center of the working-class neighborhood.
Though lacking the grandeur of private schools, Waterford Secondary School aims to provide a high-quality education to a broader range of students.

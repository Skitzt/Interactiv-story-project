Collaborate with Others or Stand Alone

Oliver faces yet another crossroads on his journey - to collaborate with like-minded individuals or continue his fight alone.
Understanding the importance of collective action, Oliver recognizes that the strength of unity lies in its ability to dismantle the walls of inequality.
He must decide whether to prioritize his personal ambition or embrace the power of solidarity.

Oliver reflects on the significance of teamwork and collaboration in addressing society's core challenges.
Recognizing that no single person can single-handedly dismantle the structures of oppression, he seeks to forge alliances with those who share his vision.
The consequences of his choice are profound - collaboration magnifies the impact of their collective efforts, while standing alone risks diluting his purpose and stunting the trajectory of change.

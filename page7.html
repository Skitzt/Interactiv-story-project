Advocate for Social Change or Accept the Status Quo

Oliver faces a new challenge - whether to advocate for social change or conform to the existing class system.

Supported by relevant facts and statistics on social inequality data from statista reveals that only 23% of students from disadvantaged backgrounds in the UK have access to private education, further entrenching the class divide.

Oliver contemplates the consequences of his choice. Advocating for social change entails raising awareness about poverty and inequality, challenging the status quo, and fighting for a fairer society.
